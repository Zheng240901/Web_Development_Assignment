Infinity Gaming and Beyond

Dive into a whole new gaming experience with ONYX – THUNDER’s premium gaming laptop to join the range of bezel-less tech with its 15.6” INFINIVISION display geared with 9th generation Intel Core processors and Nvidia RTX graphics that remain cool within an impressively slim frame. Weighing in at only 1.95kg, this ultra-slim beast doesn’t just bring unparalleled performance for its size but it ups the ante with a state-of-the-art GENESWITCH mechanical keyboard to give you the full gaming experience you can carry with you – anytime, anywhere.

Continue to Push The Boundaries with

INFINIVISION Bezel-less Display

Experience gaming like never before with THUNDER’s INFINIVISION display – for gaming that explodes beyond the screen.
INFINIVISION also reduces the form factor of ONYX, transforming a 15.6” look to a sleek 14” appearance.

Feather-light Portability

Ultra-slim and ultra-powerful, ONYX’s anodized aluminum body is only 19.9mm thin and weighing at only a mere 1.95kg for a 15.6” full-featured gaming laptop. It’s a compact powerhouse that fits snugly into your backpack being half the weight and size of your usual gaming laptop.

Heavy-weight Gaming Performance with The 9th Gen

Intel Core i7 Processor

Light in weight but heavy in performance, ONYX can be configured with 9th Generation Intel Core i7 processor – the latest processor currently available in the arena of gaming laptops with the capacity to sport up to 32GB RAM – all designed to support smooth multitasking, photo editing and 4K video editing.
Everything done all at once. Non-stop.

RTX. IT’S ON.

Configure your ONYX with NVIDIA GeForce RTX graphics to power your gaming with graphics that match the action, creating a whole new cinematic and visual experience to add more realism to every game.
ONYX also equipped with an automatic GPU Boost feature which allows your system to overclock dynamically to make the most out of every ounce of computing power available. This feature enables maximized processing, taking your graphics performance to a whole new level.

Feel Your Every Move with

Mechanical Keyboard v2
by GENESWITCH

Turn up your gaming experience with THUNDER’s mechanical keyboard v2 by GENESWITCH for gaming you can actually feel.
The GENESWITCH mechanical keyboard is an improved mechanical keyboard which delivers incredible tactility, faster responsiveness, 2.8mm key travel distance, larger keycap, and longer life span (50 million clicks).

Eye-opening 144Hz
IPS sRGB 100% Display

See your game’s true colours with ONYX’s IPS Display with sRGB 100% color gamut and wide-angle viewing – the ideal set up to satisfy your hunger for eye-popping and surreal visuals.
If that’s not enough, upgrade it to 144Hz IPS Display to power fast-paced gameplays with more responsive refresh rates for higher accuracy and zero lag or motion blur – all in the name of smoother and clearer graphics.

Only Your Game Stays Hot

ONYX’s superior cooling system is made up of quad heat pipes, quad-vent & heatsink, dual-fan – an unrivaled system that maintains your machine’s cool even when your gameplay gets intensely hot and heavy. ONYX is also built with an anodized aluminum chassis to further support its cooling system with an effective heat dissipation.

Cutting Edge Connectivity with WiFi 6

ONYX is equipped with the latest Intel & Killer AX wireless cards, making it the first laptop in MALAYSIA to be WiFi 6 ready. WiFi 6 is set to gradually replace the current generation of WiFi 5 and promises enhanced connection stability, lower power consumption and a host of efficiency upgrades ideal for professionals who need to stay connected and productive at all times. In other words, WiFi 6 is the future standard for wireless connectivity, and the good news is, your ONYX is already there.

Take Full Control

Manage your system from the convenience of a unified single interface via the THUNDER Command Center. This all-in-one software allows you to monitor your system information such as temperature and network speed as well as make finer adjustments to areas including fan speeds, keyboard backlight colors and more. It also provides quick setting options where the user can switch between different usage modes to optimize power consumption.
