Triple Your Gaming Experience

TROIKA redefines the gaming experience with its out-of-this-world V-shape, tri-cabin casing that separates a gaming PC’s most heat generating parts into three compartments with individual airducts for a more effective cooling system in order to power longer hours of gaming and performance. TROIKA is every gamer’s dream machine not just for its cool aesthetics but for its incredible customization options. It can support up to RTX2080Ti full-length graphics, impeccable SSD and Hard Drives options, including the selection of Intel or AMD system setup, as well as a water-cooling setup.
