Set Your Gaming Foundation Right

Introducing MATMASTER, THUNDER’s first gaming mouse mat made to improve your comfort and agility during gameplay. Matmaster comes in 2 editions: speed or control – both built to last against water and other extreme gaming wear and tear.

Choose SPEED

The Matmaster Speed edition uses high-quality polyester materials treated with friction-reducing coating to allow you to glide through your game with lightning fast mouse movements.

Choose CONTROL

The Matmaster Control edition uses high-quality jacquard fabric coated with the perfect amount of surface friction and texture to ensure every movement is conscious and precise. Suitable for FPS gamers looking for extreme control and accuracy.

Built for

Intensity

The Matmaster is made to resist the stressors of extreme gaming conditions with its stress-resistant and water-resistant build – hardy against spilled drinks and intense gameplays.

Built for

Long-lasting Comfort

The Matmaster features a stitched border to prevent fraying edges over time, plus a thick 5mm padding with rubberized backing for maximum comfort and no-slip grip even on uneven surfaces.
