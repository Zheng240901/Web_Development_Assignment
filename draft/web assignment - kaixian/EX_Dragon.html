EX Dragon

Heavy on Performance

Light on Weight

They say superior performance usually comes with a tradeoff – added weight and a bulkier design. This is not the case at all with EX DRAGON.

This gaming powerhouse barely tips the scale at only 1.95kg but packs a mean punch in the gaming arena.

Uncover Endless Possibilities with

INFINIVISION Bezel-less Display

Get the most out of every game or task with our trademarked INFINIVISION bezel-less display*, a must-have for every hardcore gamer.

The maximized bezel-less design allows for a 15.6” Full HD screen to be compactly housed in a sleek 14” body.

Reign Supreme – Unleash the Power of

9th Gen. Intel i7 Processors

Enjoy desktop-level processing power with the latest 9th Generation Intel Core i7

These state-of-the-art CPUs provide massive firepower to take on both extreme multitasking and intense gaming.

EX DRAGON's RAM is also expandable up to 32GB to meet future needs.

A Whole New Level of Gaming Experience Awaits

Experience every game the way it should be experienced – in its full graphics glory. EX DRAGON is powered by the latest NVIDIA GeForce GTX1650 graphics cards capable of producing stunning media-rich graphics for the latest and some of the most demanding game titles.

Absolute Clarity

Unrivaled Gameplay

See it all and play better, no matter how visually diverse the game is.

A quantum leap forward from the traditional 60Hz display, EX DRAGON's superior 144Hz* screen makes it the ideal gaming laptop for dynamic games with fluid, fast-moving visuals.

Play Hot | Stay Cool

Integrated with a specialized cooling system, EX DRAGON offers double the cooling performance with its dual heat pipes (*EX's triple head pipes), dual-fan and dual-vent design. This optimized system ensures effective heat dissipation even for the hottest game titles.

Storage That Suits Your Needs

Whether you’d like to stay lean and mean with SSD, go big and bold with HDD, or both, EX DRAGON is ready to accommodate your preference. DRAGON comes with two M.2 PCIe SSD and one HDD slots, giving you the option to expand your storage up to 6TB.

Optimize Your Gaming

Maximize Your Experience

EX DRAGON comes equipped with 2x Mini Displayports and 1x HDMI port, enabling extension to 3x additional screens.

It is also equipped with a USB Type-C connector to support modern devices and peripherals.

Fully Optimized for All-out Gaming

EX DRAGON runs on Windows 10, the ideal operating system for gaming. The system allows DRAGON to not only carry a large majority of game titles, but also take on extreme as well as complex gaming conditions smoothly.
