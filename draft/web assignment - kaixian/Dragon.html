Heavy on Performance

Light on Weight

They say superior performance usually comes with a tradeoff – added weight and a bulkier design. This is not the case at all with DRAGON.

This gaming powerhouse barely tips the scale at only 1.95kg but packs a mean punch in the gaming arena.

Reign Supreme – Unleash the Power of

9th Gen. Intel i5 Processors

Enjoy desktop-level processing power with the latest 9th Generation Intel Core i5

These state-of-the-art CPUs provide massive firepower to take on both extreme multitasking and intense gaming.

DRAGON's RAM is also expandable up to 32GB to meet future needs.

A Whole New Level of Gaming Experience Awaits

Experience every game the way it should be experienced – in its full graphics glory. DRAGON is powered by the latest NVIDIA GeForce GTX1050 graphics cards capable of producing stunning media-rich graphics for the latest and some of the most demanding game titles.

Ultimate Precision at Your Fingertips

DRAGON's specialized keyboard comes with a wealth of gamer-oriented features including N-key rollover, anti-ghosting, customizable per-key RGB lighting and outstanding durability (50 million clicks).

DRAGON further sets itself apart with its v2 GENESWITCH mechanical keyboard* which provides that crisp, unmistakable tactile feel for superior responsiveness and gameplay.

Play Hot | Stay Cool

Integrated with a specialized cooling system, DRAGON offers double the cooling performance with its dual heat pipes, dual-fan and dual-vent design. This optimized system ensures effective heat dissipation even for the hottest game titles.

Storage That Suits Your Needs

Whether you’d like to stay lean and mean with SSD, go big and bold with HDD, or both, DRAGON is ready to accommodate your preference. DRAGON comes with two M.2 PCIe SSD and one HDD slots, giving you the option to expand your storage up to 6TB.

Fully Optimized for All-out Gaming

DRAGON runs on Windows 10, the ideal operating system for gaming. The system allows DRAGON to not only carry a large majority of game titles, but also take on extreme as well as complex gaming conditions smoothly.

Get into The Game

Equipped with two high-performance speakers and a subwoofer*, DRAGON produces a gripping audio experience that pulls you into the heart of the action.

DRAGON is also powered by SoundBlaster Cinema 5 audio enhancement suite, making the experience even more immersive.
